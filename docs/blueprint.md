# **App Name**: SkinVision AI

## Core Features:

- Image Upload/Capture: Enable users to upload skin images for analysis or capture directly through their device's camera.
- AI-Powered Detection: Use AI algorithms to detect potential skin diseases from uploaded images. The system will employ reasoning to use patient details as a tool during the diagnosis phase.
- Real-Time Results Display: Show detection results immediately after analysis, including the identified condition and severity level.
- Personalized Care Suggestions: Generate custom care suggestions based on the AI's findings.
- AI-Generated Recommendations: Provide AI-driven medicine recommendations, prevention tips, and follow-up guidance for identified conditions.
- Landing Page with Information: Create an engaging landing page that explains the platform's features and guides users.
- Responsive Design: Ensure the platform is fully responsive and performs well across all devices.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Calm blue (#5DADE2), conveying trust and health. 
- Background color: Soft, desaturated blue (#EBF5FB) for a clean and modern feel.
- Accent color: Complementary light green (#A3E4D7) for positive guidance and actions.
- Font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font known for its readability and clean, modern aesthetic; suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Use clear, minimalist icons related to health and dermatology, ensuring they are easily recognizable.
- Clean and user-friendly layout with a clear hierarchy for easy navigation. Focus on a grid-based design for consistent spacing and alignment.
- Subtle animations to guide users through the diagnosis process and provide feedback on actions.
